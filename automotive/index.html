Adapting to Market Demands

Growing Dependence on Semiconductor Technology

Over the past decade, the automotive market has aggressively increased the use of semiconductor technology. Semiconductor suppliers are adapting to these market requirements, where high-volume designs also feature long program life and aftermarket requirements. Fluctuations in manufacturing cycles and the distribution of work to subsystem manufacturers can further complicate matters.

Advanced Testing Services

Supporting the Automotive Supply Chain

At ZacTech Semi, we support the automotive semiconductor supply chain through advanced testing, analysis, and qualification services designed to meet AEC-Q100/101 standards and other industry requirements. Our lab provides ESD & Latch-Up testing, reliability qualification, failure analysis, and encapsulation evaluation, helping ensure every component performs flawlessly under the harsh conditions vehicles face — including temperature extremes, vibration, and long-term electrical stress.
