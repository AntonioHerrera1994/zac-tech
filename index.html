What We Do

Zac Tech Semi
Services

Reliability Tests
Accelerated stress testing to predict long-term device performance and lifespan under extreme conditions.
Package Qualification Testing
Comprehensive validation of semiconductor packages per JEDEC, AEC-Q and customer-specific standards.
ESD & Latch-UP
Electrostatic discharge and latch-up characterization to ensure device robustness in real-world environments.
