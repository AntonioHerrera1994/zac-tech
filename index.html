What We Do

Zac Tech Semi
Services

Reliability Tests
HTOL, LTOL, ELFR, HAST and THB — accelerated stress protocols per JEDEC JESD22 and AEC-Q100 to qualify devices before mass production.
Package Qualification Testing
Full package-level qualification combining preconditioning, thermal cycling, moisture sensitivity, and board-level solder joint reliability evaluations.
ESD & Latch-UP
HBM, CDM, and MM electrostatic discharge characterization plus latch-up testing per JEDEC JESD78 — essential for automotive and industrial robustness.
Failure Analysis
SEM, EDX, and optical microscopy for material characterization, dimensional measurement, and root-cause identification at the nanoscale.
Forensic Services
End-to-end failure investigation: electrical fault localization, selective decapsulation, cross-section analysis, and detailed engineering reports for warranty and litigation support.

At our laboratory, innovation is not just a goal — it's the foundation of everything we do. With over three decades of experience in the semiconductor industry, we transform every technological challenge into a measurable opportunity to move forward.

Fast Response in Critical Times
Production stoppages cost the industry millions per hour. Our lab runs accelerated turnaround protocols — most reliability tests can be initiated within 24 hours of sample receipt — without ever compromising measurement accuracy or traceability.
02
Personalized Technical Interface
We go beyond raw data delivery. Every report is followed by a one-on-one technical debrief with our reliability engineers, helping your team interpret Weibull plots, activation energies, and failure mechanisms — turning numbers into strategic decisions.
03
Controlled Environment Capabilities
Our ISO-certified cleanroom facilities maintain Class 10,000 (ISO 7) conditions, ensuring test samples are handled under the same environmental controls used in leading semiconductor fabs — eliminating contamination as a variable in your results.
04
Flexible Standards & Protocols
We are fluent in JEDEC, AEC-Q, MIL-STD, IEC, and proprietary OEM standards. Rather than forcing your products into rigid test templates, we design qualification flows that align with your internal requirements and customer-specific acceptance criteria.
05
Adapting to the Technological Future
From advanced packaging (3D-IC, chiplets, WLP) to 5G RF components and automotive-grade power semiconductors, we continuously expand our test methodologies to support the next generation of semiconductor technologies before they reach mass production.
