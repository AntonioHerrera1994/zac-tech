Aerospace and Defense Procurement

Extended Program Durations in Defense

The defense and aerospace industries face some of the most challenging procurement and supply chain requirements, long design cycles, and exceptionally long program durations. With more rigorous qualification testing, products from these industries face conditions that are not aligned with the general semiconductor market.
